Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

1. What courses does Caazmi Institute of Technology offer?
We offer courses like Graphic Design, Web Development (Beginner to Expert levels), and more, designed for both beginners and professionals.
2. Are the courses available online, on-campus, or both?
Currently, our courses are available on-campus, with plans to introduce online classes soon.
3. What is the duration of most courses?
The duration varies. For example, the beginner web development course is 2 months, while intermediate and advanced courses are 4 months each.
4. How can I contact Caazmi Institute of Technology for more information?
You can reach us at info@caazmisol.com or WhatsApp us at +923033511879.

Admission & Application

5. What are the eligibility criteria for enrolling in your programs?
Basic computer knowledge is sufficient for most courses. Technical courses like programming may require some prior understanding, but it's not mandatory.
6. How can I apply for a course at Caazmi Institute of Technology?
Visit our campus or contact us via email or WhatsApp for the enrollment process.

Courses & Training

7. Do you offer flexible class timings for working professionals?
Yes, we have evening and weekend classes for working individuals.
8. What technologies and tools are covered in your courses?
Our courses include tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and programming technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.
9. Do you provide practical training or internships?
Yes, our training is hands-on and project-based, with internship opportunities available.
10. Is there a certificate awarded upon course completion?
Yes, students receive a certificate of completion recognized by industry professionals.

Career Support

11. Does the institute provide any career support?
Yes, we offer career counseling, freelancing workshops, and guidance for platforms like Fiverr and Upwork.
12. Are the instructors industry professionals?
Yes, all our instructors have practical experience in their respective fields.

Fee Structure

13. What is the fee structure for the courses?
  • Beginner Web Development: PKR 8,000/month
  • Intermediate and Advanced Web Development: PKR 6,000/month
